## 2024-04-30 - Redundant TalkBack Announcements in Compose Components
**Learning:** In Jetpack Compose, components that inherently announce their state (like `FilterChip` for selection) or are immediately followed by descriptive text (like an app icon followed by the app name) often suffer from redundant TalkBack announcements if internal icons retain generic content descriptions like "Done icon" or "app icon".
**Action:** When auditing Compose UI for accessibility, proactively set `contentDescription = null` for internal, state-indicating icons within composite components (like chips) and for decorative images accompanied by text. Additionally, use `onClickLabel` in `clickable` modifiers to replace generic "Double tap to activate" prompts with context-specific actions (e.g., "Double tap to open").
