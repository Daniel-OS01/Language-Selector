## 2024-06-25 - Redundant TalkBack Announcements in Custom Composables
**Learning:** Custom composables like `QuickTextButton` and `AppListItem` that combine an `Icon`/`Image` with adjacent `Text` and wrap them in a `clickable` modifier often lead to redundant TalkBack announcements. By default, TalkBack announces the `contentDescription` of the icon, the text itself, and "Double tap to activate".
**Action:** When an `Icon` or `Image` is purely decorative or its meaning is clearly conveyed by immediately adjacent text within the same clickable container, set its `contentDescription = null` to avoid repetition. Additionally, provide a more descriptive action using `onClickLabel` on the `clickable` modifier (e.g., `clickable(onClickLabel = "Open ${app.name}")`) to give users better context than the generic "activate".
