## 2024-05-17 - Securely Disabling Android App Backups
**Vulnerability:** The application was inadvertently permitting data extraction due to incomplete backup configurations. While `android:allowBackup` was set to "true", setting it to "false" alone is insufficient on Android 12+ (API 31+) to prevent device-to-device transfers.
**Learning:** To securely and completely disable backups and data extraction on Android 12+, `android:allowBackup="false"` must be combined with explicitly excluding all domains (`root`, `file`, `database`, `sharedpref`, `external`) in both `<cloud-backup>` and `<device-transfer>` sections of `data_extraction_rules.xml`, as well as under `<full-backup-content>` in `backup_rules.xml`. Each `<exclude>` element must define both `domain` and `path` attributes.
**Prevention:** Always define explicit `dataExtractionRules` and `fullBackupContent` rules that exclude all domains when the intent is to disallow all backups and data transfers, rather than relying solely on `android:allowBackup="false"`.
