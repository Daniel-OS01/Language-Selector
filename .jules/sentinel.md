## 2024-06-17 - Securely Disable Android Data Extraction
**Vulnerability:** The application had `android:allowBackup="true"` and allowed empty configurations for `dataExtractionRules` and `backup_rules`. This configuration could permit sensitive data extraction and unintended data transfers.
**Learning:** To securely disable backups on Android 12+ (API 31+), setting `android:allowBackup="false"` is insufficient because it is ignored for device-to-device transfers. You must retain `android:dataExtractionRules` and explicitly `<exclude>` all domains (`root`, `file`, `database`, `sharedpref`, `external`) under both `<cloud-backup>` and `<device-transfer>` in `data_extraction_rules.xml`, and apply the same `<exclude>` rules under `<full-backup-content>` in `backup_rules.xml`.
**Prevention:** Always verify that both `data_extraction_rules.xml` and `backup_rules.xml` are explicitly populated with explicit paths exclusions and that `android:allowBackup` is explicitly `false` to disable data extraction on all platforms.
