## 2024-06-14 - Securely Disable Android App Backups
**Vulnerability:** The application had `android:allowBackup="true"` and no specific exclusion rules for Android 12+ backups and transfers. This could allow sensitive app data extraction via adb, cloud backups, or device-to-device transfers.
**Learning:** To securely disable backups on Android 12+ (API 31+), setting `android:allowBackup="false"` is insufficient because it is ignored for device-to-device transfers. You must retain `android:dataExtractionRules` and explicitly `<exclude>` all domains (`root`, `file`, `database`, `sharedpref`, `external`) under both `<cloud-backup>` and `<device-transfer>` in `data_extraction_rules.xml`, and apply the same `<exclude>` rules under `<full-backup-content>` in `backup_rules.xml`.
**Prevention:** Ensure new Android projects are configured with `android:allowBackup="false"` and explicitly exclude all data domains in `data_extraction_rules.xml` and `backup_rules.xml` to prevent unintended data extraction.
